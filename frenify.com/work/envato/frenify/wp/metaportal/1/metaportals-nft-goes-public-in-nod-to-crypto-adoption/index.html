TOKENOMICS

Crypto, Tokens

Token Distribution:
 60% of tokens will be reserved for presale
 15% of tokens will be reserved for CEX and Partnerships
 20% of tokens BURNED
 5% of tokens will be reserved for Team

Token Utility:
 Staking: Holders of Meme Coin will have the ability to stake their tokens, earning a percentage of the transaction fees generated on the platform.
 Payment: Meme Coin can be used as a means of payment within the platform, allowing users to purchase premium features and services.
 Rewards: Users can earn rewards for participating in the community and contributing to the platform's growth.

Token Sale:
 Public sale of Meme Coin will take place on major cryptocurrency exchanges, with a portion of the tokens reserved for private sale to accredited investors.
 Token price will be determined based on market demand and supply.
 Unsold tokens will be added to the liquidity pool for future use.

Overall, Meme Coin's tokenomics are designed to support the growth and sustainability of the platform, while providing value and benefits to its users. By incorporating a variety of use cases and rewards for participation, we believe that Meme Coin has the potential to become a popular and widely-used cryptocurrency.
